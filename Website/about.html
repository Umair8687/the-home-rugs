About Us

Excellence in Carpet & Rug Manufacturing Since 1995

Our Story

Founded in 1995, our factory has grown from a small family business into a leading manufacturer and exporter of hand-woven, hand-tufted, and machine-made carpets and rugs. Located in the heart of India's rug weaving region, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver world-class products to over 15 countries.

Quality Assurance

Quality is at the core of our operations. Every carpet undergoes rigorous inspection at each stage—from yarn selection and dyeing to weaving and finishing. We use only premium raw materials and eco-friendly dyes, ensuring durability and vibrant colors.

Certifications

  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified
  • Good Weave Member (No Child Labor)
  • Global Organic Textile Standard (GOTS)

Our Products

We offer a wide range of products from size 1 X 1 ft. to 15 X 90 ft. including hand-knotted rugs, hand-tufted rugs, machine-made rugs, doormats, area rugs, broad rolls(wall to wall) of size 15 X 90 ft. and custom designs for residential, commercial, indoor, outdoor rugs and hospitality projects.

Sustainability

We are committed to sustainable practices —using organic fibers and minimizing waste. Our factory supports local communities and provides fair wages and safe working conditions for all employees.
